About

Stephen Cline

Hello and thanks for stopping by. I am a geospatial web developer who lives in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania. By day I work for a transportation engineering company.

I enjoy mountain biking, nature, traveling and cities. I sometimes think of myself as an armchair sociologist and economist - understanding the inner workings of humans, our beliefs and behaviors. I love learning and exploring.

I have an interest in mapping, design and the web. I care about how people experience the web, in particular maps and geospatial applications. I love using Leaflet for making maps on the internet.

I created a free web app called Vector to edit GeoJSON data, an open source GIS data format.
