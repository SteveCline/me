Adventures of Dill

9/25/2024

We recently retired our trusty tent of 10 years "Big Red" after a long and prosperous life. Our new car camping tent is a dark forest green with neon green trim which we have lovingly named Dill.

We wish we could have documented all the epic trips of Big Red, but with a new tent comes a new opportunity. This article will serve as a tracker for all the adventures of our car camping tent. Long live Dill!
